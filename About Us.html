About Us

Who We Are

Webura Company Limited is a dynamic and innovative company committed to solving community needs through diverse industries. With a strong foundation in Shea butter production, livestock farming, timber production, construction services, quarry supply, and sole tape manufacturing, we strive to provide high-quality products and services that improve lives and support economic growth.

Our Mission

At Webura Company Limited, our mission is simple: to solve community needs and problems by delivering essential products and services with excellence. We believe in creating value through innovation, efficiency, and customer-focused solutions.

Our Vision

We aim to be the best supplier in our industries, setting new standards of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

  1. Innovation: We embrace creativity and technology to enhance our operations and offerings.
  2. Customer Service: We prioritize our customers' needs, ensuring they receive the best products and experiences.

Our Leadership

Founded by Apayire Bernstein Webura, Webura Company Limited is driven by a passion for business and community impact. Under the leadership of Manager Peter Aniadaga Wepare, we continue to expand and refine our operations to meet the highest standards.

Our Location

We are headquartered in Tema, Accra, Ghana, strategically positioned to serve local and international markets efficiently.

Why Choose Us?

At Webura Company Limited, customer service is our top priority. We build lasting relationships with our clients, ensuring satisfaction through high-quality products and a commitment to excellence.

Join us on our journey as we shape industries and uplift communities!
